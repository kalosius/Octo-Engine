Education Levels

O-Level (S1 - S4)

Our O-Level program provides a strong foundation in core subjects and prepares students for the Uganda Certificate of Education (UCE) examinations.

  • Duration: 4 years
  • Curriculum: Uganda National Curriculum
  • Subjects: English, Mathematics, Sciences, Social Studies, and more

A-Level (S5 - S6)

Our A-Level program offers specialized subject combinations to prepare students for university education and the Uganda Advanced Certificate of Education (UACE) examinations.

  • Duration: 2 years
  • Curriculum: Advanced level studies
  • Subject Combinations: Sciences, Arts, and Humanities
